How-To Guide

Complete guide to getting the most out of FocusHub

Getting Started

1. Choose Your Energy Level

Why energy levels matter
ADHD brains don't have consistent energy. FocusHub adapts to YOUR reality:
  • Low (15 min sprints): Dragging? Short bursts keep momentum.
  • Medium (20 min sprints): Normal days. Where consistency lives.
  • High (30 min sprints): Peak state. Maximize this window.
üí° Pro Tip: Be honest about your energy. Lying to yourself wastes time. If you're at 30% energy, choose Low and actually complete sprints.

2. Get Morning Reflection

Personalized daily motivation
After selecting energy, you'll see a reflection that:
  • References YOUR recent performance
  • Acknowledges YOUR streak (if you have one)
  • Adapts message to YOUR energy level
  • Provides spiritually-inspired wisdom (not religious)
Example for 5-day streak + high energy:
"Day 5. Peak energy. This is your moment. Attack your biggest challenge first."

Task Management

1. Brain Dump Everything

Get it out of your head
Type tasks in Brain Dump section. Don't think. Just capture.
  • Hit Enter to save
  • No organization yet
  • Just empty your brain

2. Move to Holding Area

Quick review, no commitment
Drag tasks from Brain Dump ‚Üí Holding Area when:
  • You've acknowledged it
  • It's not immediate priority
  • You'll decide later

3. Set Task Priority

Mark what matters most
Every task has a priority indicator (colored circle) next to the checkbox. Click to cycle through:
  • üî¥ High Priority: Must get done today. Gets 1.5x point multiplier.
  • üü° Medium Priority: Should get done. Standard 1.0x multiplier (default).
  • ‚ö™ Low Priority: Nice to have. Gets 0.8x multiplier.
Priority Impact on Points:
Final task value = Base points √ó Priority multiplier
  • High-priority Strategic: 1.5 √ó 1.5 = 2.25 points (maximum)
  • Medium-priority Admin: 0.5 √ó 1.0 = 0.5 points
  • Low-priority Admin: 0.5 √ó 0.8 = 0.4 points (minimum)
Why this matters: The AI tracks your priority choices. If you complete 5 low-priority tasks while ignoring high-priority ones, expect brutal feedback about avoidance behavior.

4. Prioritize Into Strategic Buckets

Three buckets, different values
Urgent Admin (0.5 points each):
  • Quick tasks, low complexity
  • Email, bills, scheduling
  • Must-do but not strategic
Deep Work (1.0 points each):
  • Focus-intensive work
  • Writing, coding, designing
  • Requires uninterrupted time
Strategic Leap (1.5 points each):
  • High-impact, business-changing
  • Client acquisition, product launch
  • Moves the needle significantly
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Priority + Category = Final Value. One high-priority strategic task (2.25 points) beats five low-priority admin tasks (2.0 points). The system prevents busywork optimization AND priority avoidance. Do what matters.

5. Set Sprint Targets

Estimate task complexity
Click up/down arrows next to üéØ icon to set expected sprint count (1-10):
  • Quick admin? 1 sprint
  • Writing a report? 3 sprints
  • Major feature? 5-10 sprints
Helps track: "This is taking way longer than expected."

Working Sessions

1. Start a Sprint

Single task focus
  1. Pick ONE task from your priority buckets
  2. Click "Start Sprint"
  3. Work ONLY on that task
  4. Timer shows time remaining

2. Handle Distractions

Track the pulls
When something pulls your attention:
  • Click "Log Distraction"
  • Type what distracted you
  • Return to work
AI tracks distraction patterns and calls you out if it's excessive.

3. Complete Tasks

Check it off
Click checkbox when done. You'll get:
  • Confetti celebration üéâ
  • Task moves to "Wins" section
  • Points added (weighted by bucket)
  • AI acknowledges progress

AI Agent & Coaching

1. Set Toughness Level

Choose your coaching intensity
Located right above AI message box:
  • üíö Gentle: Supportive, encouraging (anxiety days)
  • üíô Firm: Balanced accountability (default)
  • üî• Brutal: No sugar-coating (need kick in ass)
Preference saves. Switch anytime based on mental state.
üí° Pro Tip: Start day on Brutal. If anxiety spikes or you're struggling, drop to Gentle. Let the app adapt to YOUR needs.

2. Understand Time-Aware Feedback

AI knows what time it is
Default work hours: 9am-6pm. AI calculates:
  • Percent of workday gone
  • Expected progress (~8 sprints, ~6 task points)
  • How far behind/ahead you are
Example at 4:30pm with 2 sprints (Brutal mode):
"83% through your workday. 2 sprints. Expected 7. You're way behind. 2 hours left."

3. Track Progress with Views

Two view modes
Switch between tabs:
  • Numbers: Current stats, progress bar
  • Charts: 7-day trends, visual history

End of Day

1. Click "END DAY"

See your AI grade
AI calculates grade from:
  • Sprints completed (40 pts)
  • Weighted task points (30 pts)
  • Distraction penalty (-3 pts each)
Total converts to A-F letter grade.

2. Self-Grade

How would YOU rate today?
After seeing AI grade, pick yours (A-F):
  • Be honest
  • Don't look at AI grade
  • Trust your gut

3. See Comparison Analysis

AI reveals your blind spots
System compares your grade vs AI:
  • Aligned: Good self-awareness
  • Overestimate: Confusing effort with results
  • Underestimate: Discounting your wins (common with ADHD)
Example if you rate C but AI says A:
"You're being too hard on yourself. The data proves you did better than you think. ADHD brains often discount wins. Don't."
üí° Track Patterns: Over weeks, see if you chronically overestimate (blind spots) or underestimate (imposter syndrome). Adjust self-perception accordingly.

4. Reflect

Quick journal
Two prompts:
  • What went well?
  • What will you do tomorrow?
Brief answers. Builds pattern recognition over time.

Advanced Features

1. Customize Work Hours

Set your schedule
Open browser console (F12), run:

Replace 8 and 17 with your start/end (24-hour format). AI now judges based on YOUR hours.

2. Build Sprint Streaks

Consistency tracking

Forces daily accountability.

Common Workflows

Morning Routine

  1. Open app
  2. Choose energy level honestly
  3. Read morning reflection
  4. Brain dump any new tasks
  5. Prioritize 3-5 tasks into buckets

  2. Check if you're on pace
  3. Adjust toughness level if needed

  1. Click "END DAY"
  2. Review AI grade
  3. Self-grade honestly
  4. Read comparison analysis
  5. Write brief reflection
  6. Submit and rest

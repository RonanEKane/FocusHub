What makes FocusHub different from other productivity apps?
FocusHub doesn't pile on endless to-do lists. It limits your focus to NOW. Energy-based timer. Strategic buckets. Tough-love accountability. Built specifically for ADHD brains that wander but still want to win.
Is this really for ADHD?
Yes. Built by someone with ADHD who tried every other system and watched them fail. FocusHub accounts for time blindness, decision fatigue, hyperfocus crashes, and the constant pull of distractions. It's not inspiration. It's scaffolding.
What's the tough-love approach?
Your Daily Pace GPS tracks performance and calls out patterns. Not a cheerleader. Not a therapist. A performance coach. If you're logging 8 distractions by noon, it tells you. If you've done 6 sprints with zero output, it asks where the work is. Accountability, not coddling.
What are faith-grounded reflections?
Daily reflections draw from virtue ethics and spiritual wisdom traditions. They're not gentle affirmations. They're firm direction rooted in centuries of teaching on discipline, duty, and purpose. Standard tier includes Catholic-based reflections presented generically. Premium unlocks multi-faith options.

Yes. Lite tier is fully functional forever. Energy-based timer, task management, brain dump, distraction logging, daily grading, progress tracking. No time limits. No feature nagging. If you want reflections, streak tracking, and pattern recognition, upgrade to Standard.

How FocusHub Works

01

Morning Reflection & Energy Check

Start with a spiritual reflection (virtue-based, Catholic-focused by default). Then rate your energy level (low/medium/high). The system calculates your baseline sprint target accordingly.

02

Brain Dump & Prioritize

Dump all tasks from your brain. Organize into priority buckets: Urgent, Deep Work, Strategic, or Quick Wins. Assign priority dots (1-3) to weight importance within each bucket.

03

Run Sprints & Track Reality

Work in focused sprints (15-90 min). When distractions happen, log them â€” declare your INTENTION before following through. The AI coaches you in real-time as you track progress.

04

Get Graded & Build Streaks

End your day with an honest performance grade (A-F). The Agent delivers direct feedback. Track your streak and consistency over time. No participation trophies.

SYSTEM PROTOCOL

How It Works

STEP 01

Energy Check

Start with a spiritual reflection. Rate your energy level. System calculates your baseline sprint target.

STEP 02

Brain Dump

Dump all tasks. Organize into priority buckets. Assign weight with priority dots (1-3).

STEP 03

Run Sprints

Work in focused sprints. Log distractions. Declare intentions. AI coaches in real-time.

STEP 04

Get Graded

End with honest performance grade (A-F). Direct coaching. Track streaks. No participation trophies.

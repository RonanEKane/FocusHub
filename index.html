About the Author

Ronan E. Kane

Author of "AI for ADHD & Anxiety"

Ronan E. Kane writes for people whose brains don't cooperate with standard productivity advice.

He has ADHD. He tried the planners, the apps, the morning routines. Most of it didn't work because it assumed his brain could estimate time, start tasks without massive resistance, and maintain systems when he missed a day.

So he built different systems. External scaffolding. AI as assistive technology. Tools that survive missed days and bad weeks.

You won't find inspiration here. You'll find instructions.

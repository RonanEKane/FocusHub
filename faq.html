Frequently Asked Questions

Core Features

What is the Energy-Based Sprint System?
Choose your energy level and get sprint durations that match:
  • Low (15 min): Running on fumes? Short sprints keep you moving.
  • Medium (20 min): Solid baseline. Where most work happens.
  • High (30 min): Peak state. Maximum output window.
The timer adapts to YOUR energy, not arbitrary rules.
How do the Strategic Buckets work?
Four buckets for managing tasks:
  • Brain Dump: Quick capture. Get it out of your head.
  • Holding Area: Tasks reviewed but not prioritized yet.
  • Urgent Admin: Must-do tasks. Worth 0.5 points.
  • Deep Work: Focus-intensive work. Worth 1.0 points.
  • Strategic Leap: High-value work. Worth 1.5 points.
Drag and drop to move tasks. At end of day, uncompleted tasks auto-return to Holding Area.
What are "Task Points" vs task count?
Tasks are weighted by value to prevent busywork optimization:
  • Admin tasks = 0.5 points
  • Deep Work = 1.0 points
  • Strategic = 1.5 points
Example: 3 Strategic tasks (4.5 points) beats 8 Admin tasks (4.0 points).
This forces high-value work, not just activity.

How does time-aware coaching work?
The AI tracks time and judges accordingly:
  • Knows your work hours (default 9am-6pm)
  • Calculates expected progress: ~8 sprints/day, ~6 task points/day
  • At 4:30pm with 2 sprints? Calls out you're behind
  • At 10am with 0 sprints? Still time to recover
  • Working at 11pm? Scolds you for poor planning
Uses percent of day gone + actual vs expected to give context.

Grading & Self-Assessment

How does the grading system work?
End-of-day grade from three components:
  • Sprints (40 pts): 8+ = 40, 6+ = 30, 4+ = 20, 2+ = 10
  • Task Points (30 pts): 7+ = 30, 5+ = 20, 3+ = 15, 1+ = 10
  • Distractions (-30 pts): Each = -3 points
Total â†’ Letter: A (90+), B (80+), C (70+), D (60+), F (<60)
What is Self-Grading?
After seeing AI's grade, YOU pick yours (A-F). AI analyzes the gap:
  • Aligned: "You see clearly. This self-awareness is rare."
  • Overestimating: "Blind spot. You're confusing effort with results."
  • Underestimating: "Too hard on yourself. ADHD brains discount wins."
Tracks over time: chronic overestimator? Imposter syndrome? Calibrated?
Builds awareness of how accurately you see your performance.

Data & Privacy

Where is my data stored?
Everything in browser localStorage:
  • No servers, no accounts, no login
  • Data never leaves your device
  • Complete privacy
Can I export my data?
Currently via browser console:
  1. Open DevTools (F12)
  2. Console tab
  3. Run: console.log(localStorage)
  4. Copy data

Customization

How do I set my work hours?
Default: 9am-6pm. To customize:
  1. Open console (F12)
  2. Run: localStorage.setItem('focushub_work_hours', JSON.stringify({start: 8, end: 17}))
  3. Replace 8 and 17 with your hours (24-hour format)
  4. Refresh
AI judges based on YOUR schedule.
Can I change themes?
Click sun/moon button in header:

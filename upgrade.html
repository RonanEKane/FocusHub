Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between versions?
Lite (Free): Perfect for trying FocusHub. Unlimited sprints, core features, localStorage analytics. General reflections only.

Student ($9/month): Session-based tracking for irregular schedules. Student-specific coaching, choose reflection traditions, Supabase sync. Perfect for students with late-night study sessions.

Regular ($14/month): Clock-based professional tracking. Professional coaching, choose reflection traditions, Supabase analytics. Perfect for 9-5 professionals.

Premium ($19/month): Everything plus meeting mode, full analytics dashboard, weekly reports, data export. For power users who need advanced features.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes! Go to Settings and click "Change Plan" under Account Type. Monthly billing adjusts automatically. No data loss when switching.
What's the difference between Student and Regular?
Student: Session-based (no clock assumptions). Coaching says "Study Session 2" not "It's 3pm". Perfect for irregular schedules, cramming, late-night studying.

Regular: Clock-based (assumes 9-5 schedule). Coaching says "It's Tuesday afternoon" and tracks required sprints per day. Better for consistent work schedules.
